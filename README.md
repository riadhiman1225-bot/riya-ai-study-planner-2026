# AI Study Planner for University Students

Building AI course project

## Summary

This project is about creating an AI study planner for university students. The AI would help students organize their study schedule based on their subjects, exam dates, attendance and learning progress. It aims to reduce stress and improve time management.

## Background

Many university students find it difficult to manage their studies, especially when they have several subjects and assignments at the same time. Sometimes students spend too much time on subjects they already understand and not enough on topics they find difficult.
As a university student, I sometimes feel confused about which subject I should study first, especially during exams and assignment deadlines. This personal experience inspired me to think about an AI study planner that could help students organize their time in a better way. I believe such a tool could reduce stress and make studying more effective.

Problems this project tries to solve:

* Poor time management
* Difficulty deciding study priorities
* Last-minute exam preparation
* Uneven focus on different subjects

## How is it used?

The student opens the application and enters basic information such as subjects, exam dates, weekly study hours and topics they find difficult. The AI analyzes this information and creates a personalized study plan.

The study plan can be updated every week based on the student's progress. If the student misses study sessions or has a new assignment, the AI adjusts the schedule automatically.

The main users are college and university students, but it could also be useful for school students.

## Data sources and AI methods

Possible data sources include:

* Student's study timetable
* Exam schedule
* Assignment deadlines
* Attendance records (if available)
* Study progress entered by the student

Possible AI methods:

* Recommendation systems
* Machine learning for predicting study priorities
* Basic optimization algorithms for scheduling

The project does not require personal data from other students. Most information is entered by the user.

## Challenges

The quality of the study plan depends on the information provided by the student. If the student enters incorrect or incomplete data, the recommendations may not be very helpful.

The AI cannot replace teachers or academic advisors. It is only designed to support students in planning their studies.

Privacy is also important because student information should be stored securely.

## What next?

In the future, the project could include:

* Automatic reminders
* Integration with Google Calendar
* Progress tracking with charts
* Personalized motivational messages
* Support for multiple languages

It could also learn from previous study habits and improve its recommendations over time.
